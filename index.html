From EPIC Her to EPIC Her Navigator

A dedicated space was the first step. Guidance is the next one.

EPIC Her Navigator builds on my previous EPIC Her proposal by turning a women-centered entrepreneurship space into an interactive guide. Instead of only presenting a place to belong, this version helps students discover the right path, mentor, and first step into the ecosystem.

“Women often have to carve out their own paths. EPIC Her Navigator is designed to make that path more visible, more welcoming, and easier to enter.”
