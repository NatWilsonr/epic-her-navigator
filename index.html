How it works

From interest to participation

Instead of asking students to figure everything out alone, the tool guides them into the right opportunities with a simple, personalized onboarding flow.

01

Understand the student

A short chat asks about startup stage, blockers, support needs, and areas of interest.

02

Generate a smart path

The system maps each answer set into a founder profile, a top barrier, and a next action.

03

Recommend people and programs

Students receive tailored mentor and opportunity suggestions that feel concrete and actionable.
