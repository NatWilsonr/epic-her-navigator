How it works

A smarter first step into entrepreneurship

Many students are interested in building, but they do not know where to start, who to ask, or what opportunity fits them best. EPIC Her Navigator reduces that friction by turning uncertainty into a clear, personalized next move.

01

Detect stage and blocker

The tool asks a short set of questions to understand whether the student is exploring, validating an idea, or ready to build — and what is slowing her down most.

02

Generate a tailored path

Based on the answers, the system creates a more relevant entry point: confidence-building, skill-building, mentorship, or team formation.

03

Connect her to the ecosystem

Instead of leaving students with general information, Navigator recommends specific people, programs, and next actions inside EPIC Her and the broader EPIC Lab community.
